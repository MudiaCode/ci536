What is a Numerator?

The numerator is the number above the line in the fraction how many parts we have out of the whole.
For example:

8/10

In the fraction the numerator is 8

What is a Denominator?

The denominator is the number below the line in the fraction and shows how many equal parts in total we have.
For example:

2/6

In the fraction the denominator is 6
