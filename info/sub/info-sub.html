How do we Subtract fractions with the same denominator?

The way in which we subtract fractions with a common denominator is very similar to how we add fractions with a common denominator
Let's look at an Example!


Example 1

8/12 - 3/12 = ?


Due to the common denominator of 12 we know the answer will also have a denominator of 12

8/12 - 3/12 = ?/12


Now we are going to look at the numerator

8/12 <- 3/12 <-


Now we have located te numerator we need to complete the subraction!

8-3/12 = 5/12


Now we have the answer let put the entire equation together!

8/12 - 3/12 = 5/12
